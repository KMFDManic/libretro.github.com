RetroArch Android

This is a port of RetroArch for Android-based devices. RetroArch Android was first released to the public on 24 January 2013 [version 0.9.8]. It is currently at [version 0.9.9.4].

What do I need to use it on my Android device?

In order to use RetroArch Android, your OS version must be Android 2.3 Gingerbread or higher.

Where to get it?

RetroArch Android can be obtained from Google Play.

Alternatively, you can sideload the APK.
