RetroArch OpenPandora

This is a port of RetroArch for the OpenPandora handheld. RetroArch OpenPandora was first released to the public on 18 September 2012 [version 0.9.7]. It is currently at [version 0.9.9].

Where to get it?

RetroArch Pandora can be obtained from the official OpenPandora repo.
